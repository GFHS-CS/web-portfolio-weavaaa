Information about the use of HEV technology in motorsports

Many series plan to either implement or switch to hybrid or electric power in the near future

At the moment however, the folowing series take advantage of HEV technology:

Formula E - Sanctioned by FIA, highest class of competition for electric powered race cars. Inagural season took place from 2014-2015.

MotoE World Cup - Electric Motorcycle racing series. Inagural season took place in 2019.

National Electric Drag Racing Association - Organizes and sanctions EV drag racing events.

World Rallycross Championship - Will be converting to all electric starting with the 2020 season.
