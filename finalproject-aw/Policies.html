U.S. Policies

Provided here are U.S. Polocies regarding the ownership of HEV models

The United States

Offers tax credits between $2,500 and $7,500 per vehicle (depending on the battery capacity) for the purchase of an EV. Some states also offer HOV, Parking, or Registration privileges, as well as Emissions Inspection benefits, and Purchase Directives, Promotion Directives, or Mandates.

It is also currently planned for new legislation to be introduced that will mandate that all new car sales will be made up of zero-emissions vehicles by 2040.
